GOODDEEDS EMPOWERMENT FOUNDATION

My name is Ambassador Binta Lawrence and am the founder of the Gooddeeds Empowerment Foundation, an Institution committed to advancing sustainable development at the grassroot level in alignment with the vision of the African Union and the aspirations of Agenda 2063.
Our Foundation was established to build an inclusive, productive and compassionate society where widows and orphans are empowered to bepcome self-reliant and fully integrated into social and oeconomic life. We operate through a clearly defined governance structure led by a Board of Trustees and a professional management team to ensure transparency, accountability and measurable impact
Our mission is executed through strategic programs in agriculture and agribusiness, food production and packaging, technical and vocational education, job creation, and orphan care services. We are developing a Technical and Skills Acquisition School to equip beneficiaries with practical, income-generating competencies. Additionally, we are committed to providing safe residential care, education, and psychosocial support for vulnerable children who have lost parental care.
GOODDEEDS EMPOWERMENT FOUNDATION stands as a practical instrument for translating continental aspirations into community-level transformation. However, sustainable impact requires partnership.
We therefore invite governments, development partners, corporate organizations, and individuals to collaborate with us through funding, technical support, and strategic alliances. Together, we can transform vulnerability into opportunity and restore dignity to those most in need.
Thank you for your commitment to shared progress.
